## Their Approach (3-Step Process)

### Step 1: Initial Consultation
- Online questionnaire with comprehensive profile
- Upload 2 recent photos (front/back)
- Details include dietary preferences, goals, activity level, restrictions
- Creates blueprint for personalized meal plan

### Step 2: Customized Meal Plan
- Developed personally by Tarren McCall
- Detailed yet easy to follow
- Emailed within 3-4 days
- Ready to start immediately

### Step 3: Regular Updates
- Meal plan updates every 3-4 weeks
- Adjusts as body composition changes
- Ensures optimal ongoing results
- Purchase updates as needed
